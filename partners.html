Todd Webb, AIA

Architecture

The architect of the partnership. Todd Webb is a seasoned, award-winning architect with over 30 years designing residential architecture. He worked at several award-winning firms before starting his own practice, webb architecture + design, in 2020 — and holds that the most seasoned architect shouldn't just design a house but draw its hardest details and run its most complex parts, staying in it from first concept through construction.

Licensed in Maryland, Virginia, Illinois, Michigan, Massachusetts & North Carolina · AIA, NCARB. Recognized in Architectural Digest, Interior Design, the AIA Distinguished Building & Small Project Awards, and the IIDA Red Award.

How It Works

Built together before they built for themselves.

The partnership started on a job, not in a boardroom. Todd first hired Steve to build a modern jewelbox renovation — choosing him for his hands-on involvement, his fluency with modern detail, and a rare willingness to get deep into the drawings before the first wall went up.

"Many architects and contractors foster animosity during construction," Todd says. "By the end, we'd truly worked together to create something special." They've built each other's work ever since — proof against each other on real, finished homes, long before they formalized it as Distinct Build.

Now they apply that same standard to homes they develop themselves — architect-grade houses, made without compromise, each one distinct.
