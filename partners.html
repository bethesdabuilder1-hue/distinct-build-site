The Partners

Two disciplines. One table.

Todd Webb designs. Steve Howard builds. They learned each other's standards the only way that sticks — on real projects, where the architect's intent meets the builder's hand. Distinct Build formalizes what they already do.

Todd Webb

Architecture

The architect of the partnership. Todd trained at Dirk Denison Architects before opening his own practice, bringing a modern, site-driven sensibility to every home — shaping it from first concept through construction documents, then staying on through the build to protect the design intent.

Steve Howard

Construction

The builder the region's leading architects trust. Building custom homes since 1990, Steve carries a national AIA Honor Award and a roster that includes Olson Kundig, David Jameson, Allan Greenberg, Dirk Denison, MODE4, and MKCA — the construction discipline that turns ambitious drawings into precisely built reality.

How It Works

Built together before they built for themselves.

Steve has built homes Todd designed — the architect's intent and the builder's hand already proven against each other on real, finished work. That history is the foundation of Distinct Build: a partnership that doesn't need a trial run, because the trial run already happened.

Now they apply the same standard to homes they develop themselves — architect-grade houses, made without compromise, each one distinct.
