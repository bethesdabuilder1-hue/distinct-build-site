The Premise

Most spec homes are a compromise by design. This is the opposite.

A developer chases margin, design becomes an afterthought, and whatever architecture survives gets value-engineered away before the drywall goes up. Buyers can feel it, even when they can't name it.

Distinct Build is what happens when the architect and the builder develop for themselves instead of for a client — homes built to the standard they'd demand on any commission, made as singular works rather than product. The name says it: each one is distinct — a designed object, not a unit.

Why The Pairing Matters

When the architect and the builder are the developer, nothing gets stripped on the way to the door.

01

Design, preserved end to end

When the architect and builder are co-owners, no one is incentivized to strip the design for margin. The vision that makes the house worth it is the vision that gets built.

02

Architect-grade, turnkey

Design at this level normally means commissioning an architect and waiting years. Here it arrives finished — the rarest thing on the market, ready to live in.

Custom-Home Standards

Modern mountain homes with custom-home bones.

A Distinct Build home is developed before you arrive — but it isn't built like a typical spec house. We bring custom-home standards into every decision, from the foundation to the finish.

Built for the Mountain

Foundation drainage, drained-and-dried walls, and standing-seam, mountain-weather detailing.

Built for Comfort

Stego vapor control, sub-slab and spray-foam insulation, and Pella Impervia windows.

Real Materials

Real wood floors, real tile, curbless showers, and solid hardware — not plastic LVP.

Custom-Home Craft

In-house millwork to our own profiles, with kitchens and baths treated as architecture.

No upgrade package. No builder-grade shortcuts. Just a better standard from the start.
