Custom-Home Standards

Modern mountain homes with custom-home bones.

A Distinct Build home is developed and finished before you arrive — and built to the standard a high-end custom builder would hold, in every decision from the foundation to the finish.

Built for the Mountain

Foundation drainage, drained-and-dried walls, and standing-seam, mountain-weather detailing.

Built for Comfort

Stego vapor control, sub-slab and spray-foam insulation, and Pella Impervia windows.

Real Materials

Real wood floors, real tile, curbless showers, and solid hardware — not plastic LVP.

Custom-Home Craft

In-house millwork to our own profiles, with kitchens and baths treated as architecture.

No upgrade package. No builder-grade shortcuts. Just a better standard from the start.
